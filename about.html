The Vibrant story

Vibrant Juice Co. was founded in Long Beach by two lifelong friends committed to healthy living and a passion for juicing. We recognized that a deficienty in essential vitamins, minerals and enzymes can have a huge impact on overall health, ability to fight disease, inflammation, brain function, immunity and much more. Raw fruits and vegetables are packed with the nutrients the body needs. Vibrant juices offer a delicious, easy and affordable way for you to incorporate more of these foods into your diet.
